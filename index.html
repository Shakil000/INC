Interscience Technologies Inc was incorporated on June 10, 2021. Interscience Technologies Inc. distributes laboratory equipment and supplies. The Company is currently being operated remotely and maintains a virtual office in Dallas, Texas. Interscience Technologies Inc. is currently distributing products to Bangladesh. Interscience Technologies serves a wide range of laboratories and research facilities.

The Company is currently being operated remotely with the support of equipment suppliers and freight forwarders. The Company procures equipment and supplies from well-recognized manufacturers. Interscience Technologies Inc. distributes equipment and supplies in the following categories to its global customers.
